How we got here
Solved · 2012–2024
Humans discover products through search and ads
Google, social, paid media. Analytics platforms track the full journey. Amplitude built the gold standard for understanding this behavior.
Happening now · 2025–2026
AI mediates discovery — and starts acting on behalf of users
ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini answer questions that used to drive website visits. AI crawlers index content to decide what gets surfaced. 38% of web traffic is now bots. AI-referred traffic doubled in Q4 2025. Amplitude's AI Visibility tracks brand mentions — but the crawl, the referral, and the conversion are still invisible.
Emerging · 2026+
AI agents browse, transact, and make decisions on websites
OpenAI's Operator, Google's agent mode, Chrome's WebMCP standard (live March 10). Agents are beginning to search products, compare prices, and complete tasks. $262B in AI-influenced retail in holiday 2025 alone. Agent commerce isn't coming — it's here.
You wrote that the feedback loop is the last bottleneck. But if the loop only captures human behavior — and a growing share of product interaction is AI-mediated — the loop itself has a blind spot. The question isn't just "what did the user do?" It's becoming "what did the AI do before the user got there?"
Why this is Amplitude's opportunity
Self-improving products need to
understand the full user journey.
AI is now part of that journey.
01 — EXTEND AI VISIBILITY
From mentions to the full AI layer
AI Visibility tracks what AI says about brands. Extend it to track AI crawlers visiting sites, AI-referred traffic converting, and which content AI chooses to surface. The chain from mention → crawl → conversion only works if both ends live in one platform.
27K brands already on AI Visibility → natural expansion path
02 — AGENT ANALYTICS
Treat AI agents as a new user type
GPTBot, ClaudeBot, Operator — these aren't just crawlers. They're attempting tasks on behalf of real users. Track agent sessions the way you track human sessions — funnels, drop-offs, success rates. Same event model. New property: source = ai_agent.
25% of Amplitude's own queries come from AI agents — the pattern is already inside the building
03 — AI-DRIVEN COMMERCE
The transaction layer is shifting
AI-influenced retail hit $262B last holiday. Agent commerce is emerging — Operator, Gemini, Perplexity are browsing and buying. Amplitude's customers (Burger King, Under Armour, Square) need to understand this traffic. It converts differently. It behaves differently.
04 — THE MOAT IS DATA
Behavioral data is the defensible layer
You've said it: "It won't be about the best ML models — it'll be about proprietary datasets." Amplitude has the deepest behavioral dataset in the industry. Adding the AI interaction layer to that dataset compounds the moat.
Pendo shipped Agent Analytics in December 2025. Cloudflare launched AI Crawl Control. The "agentic search" category your team mentioned at the TMT conference — this is the space. And Amplitude's depth makes it the natural owner.
